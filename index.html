Social Account Removal Guide

Step-by-step guides to remove your accounts from major platforms.

⚠️ Important: Deleting an account does not guarantee immediate removal of all personal data. Most platforms retain some information in backups and logs for a period of time.

Facebook

  1. Go to Settings & Privacy
  2. Click “Settings”
  3. Select “Accounts Center”
  4. Go to “Personal details” → “Account ownership and control”
  5. Click “Deactivation or deletion”
  6. Select your account and choose “Delete account”

What happens after deletion:

  • Account is scheduled for deletion immediately
  • Full deletion can take up to 30 days
  • Some data may remain in backups longer
  • You can cancel deletion by logging back in within 30 days
Go to Facebook Settings

Instagram

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Open Settings
  3. Go to Accounts Center
  4. Select “Personal details”
  5. Tap “Account ownership and control”
  6. Select “Deactivation or deletion”

What happens after deletion:

  • Your account is permanently deleted after 30 days
  • You can cancel deletion by logging back in within that period
  • Your photos, followers, and messages are permanently removed
  • Your username may not be available again

Difficulty: Medium

Time required: 2–5 minutes

TikTok

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Tap the menu (☰)
  3. Go to Settings and privacy
  4. Select “Account”
  5. Tap “Deactivate or delete account”
  6. Follow confirmation steps

What happens after deletion:

  • Your account is deactivated for 30 days before permanent deletion
  • You can reactivate it anytime during that period
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted
  • Some data may remain in backups temporarily

Difficulty: Easy

Time required: 1–3 minutes

Open TikTok Settings

X (Twitter)

  1. Go to Settings and privacy
  2. Select “Your account”
  3. Click “Deactivate your account”
  4. Read info and confirm
  5. Enter password to complete

What happens after deletion:

  • Your account is deactivated for 30 days before permanent deletion
  • You can restore it by logging in during that time
  • After 30 days, your account and data are removed
  • Your username may become available again

Difficulty: Easy

Time required: 1–3 minutes

Go to X Settings
